Who are we?
We are digital nomads, working on-the-go. We met in person many times but we collaborate exclusively on-line. Without personal contact we (Agile Hardware) wouldn’t exist for sure. We share common opinions, have been discussing cryptocurrencies many time and now we decided to act and join the best cryptocurrencies of them all – Cardano. We truly believe that engagement with academic world is the key to success. We are passionate but patient. We know that great things deserve as much time as necessary to manifest. We are software engineers so we know from the first hand how hard is it to create effective, sustainable, multi platform and secure software. We love how active the development of Cardano is and this is our first step into the ecosystem.

Why Cardano?
Cardano was easy choice once we decided to work full time for a cryptocurrency. However we also want to join the community because there is so much code which needs to be created here. Not like Bitcoin where there is so much “noise”, “maximalism” and toxicity in general. We still love Bitcoin though, he is the king because Bitcoin in general is the first “digital gold” ever created. It’s code is stored unbelievable securely “written in the stone” for more than 1000yrs which proves how important Bitcoin is. It’s really incredible human achievement, probably as incredible as the Internet itself. The Blockchain (the ledger ) is in wat “written in stone” too, because it is permanent and cannot be changed thanks to Bitcoin’s protocol design, mining and nodes running around the world 24/7. No government or agency can turn it off. It’s inflation by mining is steady and almost everything what was promised was already achieved. Maybe only things what Bitcoin ecosystem miss are. True decentralization and true scaling – we believe that the Cardano will deliver both!
