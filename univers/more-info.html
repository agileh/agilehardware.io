Agile Hardware
 
 
 
 
We believe that hardware in general should be as “agile” as possible meaning that customizable matching any current needs and expectations easily and rapidly. This can be achieved by software - the “brain” of the Hardware. Open source software is the way to go and we feel it deep in our hearts. Everyone should be able to look under the software’s hood – inner working meaning the code. In the ideal world even hardware, it’s production schemes and inner workings even the design should be open sourced. We believe that we are slowly getting there.
MORE
 
 
 
 
Bitcoin is an example how the hardware became more agile, open and useful. It wasn’t possible to transfer value – digital gold mined by miners - in truly sovereign way before it’s inception. Bitcoin is digital gold and it’s beautiful. Decentralization is the key element of sovereign – unstoppable – cryptocurrency. However it’s level is fluctuating in time. Decentralization itself is complex and multifaceted concept that spans fiscal, political, and administrative dimensions. Now with the creation of the Bitcoin it including informatics, cryptography etc. This is in our humble opinion proof that the creation of souvereign and truly decentralized “crypto” economy is possible. Cardano is confidently aiming there.
