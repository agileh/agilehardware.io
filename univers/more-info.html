Who are we?
We are software engineers so we know from the first hand how hard is it to create effective, sustainable, multi platform and secure software. We decided that our journey to the world of decentralized money should begin with Cardano. We love how active the development of Cardano is and we truly believe that engagement with academic world is the key to success. We are passionate but patient. We know that great things deserve some time to manifest. We can't stand aside anymore!

Bitcoin?
We were mining Bitcoin a little back in the 2016 with few ASICs. We were passionate about mining and about cryptocurrencies in general ever since. However seeing how it’s more and more unrealistic to mine at home was quite sad. Huge mining operations took the joy of mining from us. Mining by huge data centers with access to cheap electricity was inevitable. We are not “Bitcoin maximalists”, maybe simply because of the fact that we had to turn our ASICs off. After the DAO hack we felt strongly that “code is the law” should matter and that was first time we heard about Charles Hoskinson a we share his vision ever since.

Agile Hardware?
We believe that hardware in general should be as “agile” as possible meaning that customizable matching any current needs and expectations easily and rapidly. This can be achieved by software - the “brain” of the Hardware. Software shouldn’t be bounded in any way. Open source software is the way to go and we feel it deep in our hearts. There should be no black boxes, everyone should be able to look under the software’s hood – inner working meaning the code. In the ideal world even hardware, it’s production schemes and inner workings even the design should be open sourced. We believe that we are slowly getting there.

Bitcoin is an example how the hardware became more agile, open and useful. It wasn’t possible to transfer value – digital gold mined by miners - in truly sovereign way before it’s inception. Bitcoin is digital gold and it’s beautiful. Decentralization is the key element of sovereign – unstoppable – cryptocurrency. However it’s level is fluctuating in time. Decentralization itself is complex and multifaceted concept that spans fiscal, political, and administrative dimensions. Now with the creation of the Bitcoin it including informatics, cryptography etc. This is in our humble opinion proof that the creation of souvereign and truly decentralized “crypto” economy is possible. Cardano is confidently aiming there.

Why Cardano?
Cardano was easy choice once we decided to work full time for a cryptocurrency. We also want to join the community because there is so much to look forward to. We are not here for quick gains, we want to be part of the community. We still love Bitcoin though, he is the king because Bitcoin in general is the first “digital gold” ever created. It really is the incredible human achievement, probably as incredible as the Internet itself. The problem is that almost everything what was promised was already achieved. Maybe only things that Bitcoin ecosystem miss are true decentralization and true scaling – we believe that Cardano will deliver both!
