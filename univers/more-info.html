Who are we?
We are digital nomads, working on-the-go. We met in person many times but we collaborate exclusively on-line. Without personal contact we (Agile Hardware) wouldn’t exist for sure. We share common opinions, have been discussing cryptocurrencies many time and now we decided to act and join the best cryptocurrencies of them all – Cardano. We truly believe that engagement with academic world is the key to success. We are passionate but patient. We know that great things deserve as much time as necessary to manifest. We are software engineers so we know from the first hand how hard is it to create effective, sustainable, multi platform and secure software. We love how active the development of Cardano is and this is our first step into the ecosystem.
Why Cardano?
Cardano was easy choice once we decided to work full time for a cryptocurrency. We also want to join the community because there is so much to look forward to. We are not here for quick gains, we want to be part of the community. We still love Bitcoin though, he is the king because Bitcoin in general is the first “digital gold” ever created. It really is the incredible human achievement, probably as incredible as the Internet itself. The problem is that almost everything what was promised was already achieved. Maybe only things that Bitcoin ecosystem miss are true decentralization and true scaling – we believe that Cardano will deliver both!
